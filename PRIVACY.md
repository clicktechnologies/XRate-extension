# Privacy Policy — Click XRate

_Last updated: 5 July 2026_

Click XRate does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal or user data.

## What the extension stores

The extension saves the following **locally on your device only**, using the browser's storage:

- your settings (enabled/disabled state, selected rate source, chosen target currencies);
- a cached copy of the latest exchange rates, so conversions work offline.

This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

## Network requests

The extension fetches official public exchange rates only from the source you select:

- the European Central Bank (ecb.europa.eu);
- the Central Bank of Russia (cbr.ru).

These requests contain no personal data and are used solely to retrieve exchange rates.

## Selected text

When you select an amount on a web page, the extension reads only that selected text to perform the conversion, entirely on your device. It does not read, collect, or transmit page content, browsing history, or any personal information.

## Analytics & tracking

The extension contains no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising.
